Company Goal

Aiyadi (Shandong) Machinery Manufacturing Co., Ltd., is located in Dongying, processing and the manufacturing base in Shandong province and a central city in the Yellow River delta.

The business scope of our company includes: design and manufacture of wood-based panel machinery and equipment, domestic manufacture of German wood-based panel production line (DIEFFENBACHER, SIEMPELKAMP) machinery and equipment and spare parts, and precision machinery parts processing.
Our company has nearly 20 experienced professional technical personnel, with many years of durable design, development and manufacturing experience, equipped with machining center, CNC machine tools, electrical processing machine tools and large-scale machine tools based on precision machining equipment.

Design and manufacturing is the key to ensure the quality of products, and our company has a complete quality assurance system and r & d capabilities, strong engineering and technical team, have rich experience in the design and manufacturing of non-standard equipment and spare parts.
